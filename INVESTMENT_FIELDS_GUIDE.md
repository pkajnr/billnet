# Investment-Critical Fields by Post Type

## Overview
Each post type now includes specific fields and supporting documents designed to help investors evaluate opportunities and make informed decisions.

---

## 1. IDEA POSTS (Seeking Funding)

### Investment Fields
| Field | Type | Purpose | Example |
|-------|------|---------|---------|
| Market Size | Text | Show total addressable market | "$2B mobile gaming market" |
| Target Audience | Text | Define customer segment | "18-35 tech-savvy gamers in US" |
| Revenue Projection | $ | Expected annual revenue | "$500,000" |
| Break-Even Months | Months | Time to profitability | "18" |
| Team Size | People | Workforce needed | "5" |
| Website | URL | Online presence | "https://myidea.com" |

### Supporting Documents
- **Business Plan** (PDF/DOCX)
  - Detailed strategy and execution plan
  - Shows feasibility and thought-through approach
  
- **Market Analysis** (PDF/Excel)
  - Market research and validation
  - Competitor analysis
  - Growth projections
  - **Why it matters:** Demonstrates market viability

### Investor Benefits
✅ Clear understanding of market opportunity
✅ Visibility into financial projections
✅ Team capability assessment
✅ Risk assessment via timeline
✅ Documented market research

---

## 2. BUSINESS POSTS (Established Business for Sale)

### Investment Fields
| Field | Type | Purpose | Example |
|-------|------|---------|---------|
| Years Active | Years | Business track record | "7" |
| Annual Revenue | $ | Current revenue stream | "$1,200,000" |
| Employee Count | People | Operational scale | "15" |
| Growth Rate | % | Year-over-year growth | "25%" |

### Supporting Documents
- **Tax Returns** (PDF/Excel)
  - Verified financial history
  - Credibility and legitimacy
  - Tax compliance proof
  
- **Business License** (PDF/Image)
  - Legal operating authority
  - Business name verification
  - **Why it matters:** Proves legitimate, operating business

- **Financial Statements** (PDF/Excel)
  - Balance sheet
  - Income statement
  - Cash flow statement
  - **Why it matters:** Complete financial picture

### Investor Benefits
✅ Proven revenue track record
✅ Existing customer base (implicit)
✅ Verified profitability
✅ Clear growth trajectory
✅ Operational viability confirmed
✅ Legal compliance verified

---

## 3. SHARE POSTS (Company Shares Available)

### Investment Fields
| Field | Type | Purpose | Example |
|-------|------|---------|---------|
| Company Valuation | $ | Total company worth | "$5,000,000" |
| Equity Offering | % | Percentage being sold | "10%" |
| Price Per Share | $ | Cost per share | "$100" |
| Total Shares Offered | # | Total shares available | "50,000" |

### Supporting Documents
- **Financial Statements** (PDF/Excel)
  - Income statement
  - Balance sheet
  - Cash flow analysis
  - **Why it matters:** Shows company financial health
  
- **Investor Deck** (PDF/PowerPoint)
  - Company overview and mission
  - Product/service explanation
  - Market opportunity
  - Financial projections
  - Use of funds
  - Team credentials
  - **Why it matters:** Comprehensive investment pitch
  
- **Cap Table / Legal Docs** (PDF/Excel)
  - Current shareholder structure
  - Existing investments
  - Legal agreements
  - **Why it matters:** Transparency on ownership

### Investor Benefits
✅ Clear valuation basis
✅ Precise equity percentage
✅ Easy price per share understanding
✅ Transparent ownership structure
✅ Professional pitch documentation
✅ Legal framework transparency

## Credibility Pyramid

```
WITHOUT Documents + Fields
└─ Basic text description only
   └─ High skepticism
   └─ Lower investment likelihood

WITH Type-Specific Fields
└─ Financial metrics visible
└─ Medium confidence
└─ Some investment interest

WITH Fields + Supporting Docs
└─ Complete picture
└─ High credibility
└─ Professional impression
└─ Maximum investment probability
```

## Usage Scenarios

### Investor Decision Making

**Scenario 1: Evaluating an Idea**
1. Read title and description
2. Check market size and target audience
3. Review financial projections and break-even time
4. Download and review business plan
5. Review market analysis for validation
6. Make funding decision

**Scenario 2: Evaluating a Business**
1. Check years active and current revenue
2. View growth rate and employee count
3. Download tax returns for verification
4. Check business license for legitimacy
5. Review financial statements for profitability
6. Make acquisition decision

**Scenario 3: Evaluating Shares**
1. Understand company valuation
2. Calculate equity stake (price × shares = total investment)
3. Review investor deck for company story
4. Check cap table for other investors
5. Review financial statements for health
6. Make investment decision

## Field Validation & Requirements

### Required vs Optional

| Post Type | Required Fields | Optional Fields |
|-----------|-----------------|-----------------|
| Idea | Title, Description, Category, Funding Goal | All investment fields, documents |
| Business | Title, Description, Category, Asking Price | All business info fields, documents |
| Share | Title, Description, Category, Price/Share, Equity% | Valuation, shares offered, documents |

### Data Type Validation

```
Market Size: String (text, no validation)
Target Audience: String (text, no validation)
Revenue Projection: Number, positive
Break-Even Months: Number, positive integer
Team Size: Number, positive integer
Website: URL format validation
Annual Revenue: Number, positive currency
Employee Count: Number, positive integer
Growth Rate: Number, 0-100%
Company Valuation: Number, positive currency
Equity Offering: Number, 0-100%
Price Per Share: Number, positive currency
Total Shares: Number, positive
```

## File Upload Specifications

### Accepted File Types
- **Documents:** PDF, DOCX, DOC
- **Spreadsheets:** XLS, XLSX (for financials)
- **Presentations:** PPTX (for decks)
- **Images:** PNG, JPEG (for licenses)

### Size Limits
- Per file: 10 MB
- Per post: 50 MB total
- Auto-generated unique filenames prevent conflicts

### File Display in Feed
- Filename with icon
- Document type label
- File size in KB
- Direct download link
- Organized by post type
